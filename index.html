About Us

In 2017, A student council (OSIS) at SMP Fastabiqul Khairat was founded to voice the student community. Formed by students, OSIS assists in a wide range of school events and activities. Students actively participate in OSIS in order to develop communication, problem–solving skills and event management. They work together with the school management to solve students’ problems and improve the school conditions in general.

OSIS has always existed since OSIS was established in the 2017 year and with it the qualities of leadership and responsibility, joined by the essence of community service and teamwork. It enables students to realise themselves as natural leaders, make inclusive and informal activities with students and the school around them and teach others through example.
